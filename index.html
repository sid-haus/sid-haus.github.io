About

Who am I? I’m a student at Rutgers University (Class of 2019, SAS Honors Program), majoring in BAIT (Business Analytics, Information Technology) and Supply Chain Management. 
But you’re probably saying that you could’ve just gone on to my LinkedIn Page and found that. That’s not why you’re here.
You’re here because you want to see what kind of person I really am.
And the truth is, I’m human. I’ve made mistakes, but I have also had the pleasure of getting wonderful opportunities from interesting people, making some of the best friends that I could hope for, and receiving help from admirable mentors.
What has all of this done for me? It has taught me to learn from my mistakes. It has made me a meticulous planner, afforded me with a sense of honesty and humility (to certain limits) , and made me and all around great person to have a cup of coffee with. 
I have a clear focus on what I want to do in life. But I also have an eclectic set of passions. Here are just some of the things that I can do/have done.

  • Web Development
  • App Development
  • Journalism
  • Analysis of Digital Technology/Disruptive Change

One more thing—I love experimenting! Designing, YouTubing, Cooking, Tutoring, Plumbing (Unsuccessfully, sorry parents!), Building/Tinkering with computers, Rooting (Android devices, not teeth), and living life to the max.
Basically, I want to matter. Have an adventure. Bask in the rays of beautiful design and art. Work on revolutionary ideas and products.
